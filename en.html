Frequently Asked Questions

What is a pentest?

A pentest, short for penetration test, is a cybersecurity assessment that simulates real-world cyberattacks to evaluate the vulnerability of a system, network, or application.

Why do I need a pentest?

You need a pentest to identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses in your IT systems before malicious actors can exploit them. This helps strengthen security and protect your data

What's the difference between a pentest and a vulnerability scan?

A vulnerability scan automatically identifies known vulnerabilities, while a pentest involves manual testing to actively exploit and validate those vulnerabilities.

Who performs the pentest?

Pentests are conducted by cybersecurity experts known as pentesters. They are experienced professionals in testing security systems.

What's the difference between an internal pentest and an external pentest?

An internal pentest simulates an attack from within your network, while an external pentest simulates an attack from outside your network.

How long does it take to complete a pentest?

The time to complete a pentest depends on the scope and complexity of the test and can range from a few days to several weeks.

What should I expect in a pentest report?

A pentest report should include a detailed description of the vulnerabilities found, associated risks, evidence of exploitation, and recommendations for remediation.

How should I address the vulnerabilities identified in a pentest?

You should prioritize vulnerabilities based on their severity and take prompt action to address them. Implement the recommended fixes to enhance security.

Will my system be damaged during a pentest?

A pentest is designed to avoid damaging systems. Pentesters follow a predefined scope and take precautions to prevent disruptions.

How often should I conduct a pentest?

The frequency of pentests depends on your environment and threat landscape. It's advisable to conduct regular pentests, such as annually or after significant system changes.

What is the cost of a pentest?

The cost of a pentest varies depending on the scope, complexity, and duration of the test. It's important to obtain quotes from multiple security service providers.

Will my system be 100% secure after a pentest?

A pentest cannot guarantee complete security, but it significantly improves security by identifying and addressing vulnerabilities. Cybersecurity is an ongoing process.
